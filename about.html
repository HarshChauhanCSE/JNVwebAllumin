About JNV Chhotaudepur

Excellence in Education, Leadership and Character Building.

Our Legacy

Jawahar Navodaya Vidyalaya Chhotaudepur is part of the prestigious Navodaya Vidyalaya system established to provide quality education to talented rural students. Over the years, the school has produced successful professionals, officers, scientists, doctors, engineers, entrepreneurs and leaders who contribute significantly to society.
